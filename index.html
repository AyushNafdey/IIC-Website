INSTITUTION'S INNOVATION COUNCIL

Institution's Innovation Council (IIC), at the Government College of Engineering, Nagpur, known as IIC GCOEN, aims to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among students and faculty. Established under the guidance of the Ministry of Educationâ€™s Innovation Cell (MIC) and in line with the national agenda for promoting entrepreneurial thinking, IIC GCOEN provides a structured platform to develop ideas, create impactful projects, and encourage start-up culture within the institution.

VISION & MISSION

Our vision: "To be a hub of innovation and creativity, driving transformative solutions that address real-world challenges and contribute to the advancement of knowledge and technology."

Our mission:

  • Foster a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration among students, faculty, and staff.
  • Provide resources, mentorship, and support for innovative projects and startups.
  • Facilitate interdisciplinary interactions to stimulate the exchange of ideas and solutions.
  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry by facilitating partnerships and collaborations.
  • Nurture a mindset of continuous learning and adaptability in the face of technological advancements.
  • Contribute to the local and global innovation ecosystem by creating a pipeline of skilled and creative professionals.
